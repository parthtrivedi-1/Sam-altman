About Sam

Sam Altman is an American entrepreneur and investor, currently the CEO of OpenAI. He’s been involved in shaping AI policy and innovation worldwide.

Career Highlights

From co-founding Loopt to leading Y Combinator, Sam has played pivotal roles in Silicon Valley. At OpenAI, he’s pushing the boundaries of artificial general intelligence.

Fun Facts

  • Born in 1985 in Chicago.
  • He dropped out of Stanford to start his first company.
  • He loves discussing the future of humanity and technology.
